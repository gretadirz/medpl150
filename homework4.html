For photomontage 1 I decided to do climate chnage. For the background I chose snowy mountains and layered on fire towards the bottom. The fire is supposed to look like it is slowly spreading up. For the last photo I cut out a sign from a picture of a lady holding it. I changhed the opacity of the fire layer so that it blended in better with the mountains and the sign. For photomontage 2 I wanted to highlight immigration and the issues surrounding deportation in the U.S. The background I did the American flag in a rustic style. Next the shadow of people on the bottom i cut out from a larger picture of them and used multiply. I resized them so that they would fit between the stripes. The group of people that are larger is a photo that was multiplied and just put behind the smaller people. The last image I cut out the poster from a larger image. I added a photo of a push pin so that it looks like the poster is pinned onto the flag and changed the color to match better to the flag using the saturation/hue and curve.
